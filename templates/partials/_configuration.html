Processing Strategies

The enhanced aux model automatically chooses the best strategy for each tool:

  • summarize - General summarization preserving key details
  • extract_key_info - Extracts only critical information (paths, names, errors)
  • structure - Organizes lists into hierarchical format
  • analyze - Adds insights and recommendations
  • filter_relevant - Context-specific filtering
